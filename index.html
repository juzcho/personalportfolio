Mentorship Exploration Framework

  • Identify 3 areas you want to develop professionally.
  • Define 2 personal growth topics you'd like guidance on.
  • List people you admire and what qualities you want to learn from them.
  • Create a short description of the type of mentor you believe you need.
  • Draft 5 questions you would ask a potential mentor.

Small Wins You Can Achieve Today

  • Write a one-page reflection on your current strengths and goals.
  • Reach out to one person for an informational chat.
  • Journal about a challenge and what advice you'd want from a mentor.
  • Update your LinkedIn headline to reflect your direction.
  • Set a 30-day learning goal and outline 3 steps to reach it.
