Goals — Justine Cho

Clear, time-bound goals with actions and success metrics to help guide mentorship and progress.

Short-term (30 days)

  • Complete a focused Power Query course and build two transformation examples.
  • Deliver one short internal presentation to practice storytelling with data.
  • Set a daily 30-minute learning habit, tracked in a simple log.

Medium-term (3–6 months)

  • Build a portfolio dashboard showcasing modeling and storytelling skills.
  • Find a mentor and hold at least three 1:1s with actionable feedback.
  • Lead a small cross-functional analytics project from start to finish.

Long-term (12 months)

  • Transition into a senior analytics role with responsibility for modeling and mentorship.
  • Run regular knowledge-sharing sessions for the team.

Action Plan

  • Weekly: practice exercises + learning log (30 minutes/day).
  • Monthly: present progress and ask 2 mentor questions.
  • Quarterly: publish a portfolio piece and solicit feedback.

Success Metrics

  • Complete course modules and submit 2 examples (Short-term).
  • Hold 3 mentor sessions and implement 3 pieces of feedback (Medium-term).
  • Apply and interview for senior roles; lead at least one cross-functional project (Long-term).

Risks & Mitigations

  • Risk: Overcommitment — Mitigation: protect learning time in calendar.
  • Risk: Hard to find mentor — Mitigation: broaden outreach and use structured asks.
